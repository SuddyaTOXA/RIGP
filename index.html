What’s your bold vision?

Raliance’s competitive impact grant program seeks to develop and enhance the most promising, replicable practices or policies to end sexual violence in one generation.

Projects are located across the United States and U.S. Territories. Grantees have sought to develop, replicate, customize, or evaluate promising strategies. Projects have addressed a broad range of issues, types of sexual violence, ages, genders, cultures, or systems that fit within one of the funding categories.

Learn more about our grantees, their innovative projects, and how they are making an impact by using the search function.
